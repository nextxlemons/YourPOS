Welcome to the YourPOS

Simple Point of Sale Webapplication for your cafe / restaurant.

How it works(Guide):

1. Staff selects a table → a `Order tab` status flips to Occupied, an active `Order` is created (or reused).

2. Add an item with its variants.

3. Settling the bill snapshots the order into a `Bill` record.

4. The table flips back to Available, ready for the next customer.
